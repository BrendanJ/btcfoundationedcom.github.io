University Relations

The establishment of relations between the Foundation and academic institutions around the world. Work has already been started in this area with several members of our committee contacting student groups and professors in various colleges and universities.

Contact

If you are interested in the work of the committee, please keep checking the forums of the Foundation, until the committee obtains a presence on the Foundation website. If you would like to contribute to the work of the committee, we would be delighted to have you.

As a first step, fill out this form and tell us a little bit about yourself. Follow the instructions and we will soon be in touch.
